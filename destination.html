Travelogue: Destin Florida

Overview

Destin Florida, some of the most well known beaches in the US can be found here. Known for their unique sand color, which is a beautiful quarts white, and ocean waters being crystal clear and see through. This city in Florida has become a massive tourist and vacation spot leading to the birth of beautiful and exotic restaurants, sight seeing locations, and games for all.

Must-See Spots

  1. Henderson Beach State Park
  2. Harborwalk Village
  3. Crab Island Sandbar
  4. Destin Commons
  5. Track Family Fun Park

Visitor Tips

  • Spots on the beaches will be grabbed quick, the earlier you can claim a spot the better.
  • Due to Destin being so popular everything from the locations to stay at to the places to eat are pricey
  • Be careful in the water, unlike some beaches on the east coast Florida weather can be severe and the waves show that.
  • You do not need to spend money to have fun, the attractions are nice however the beaches are the reason Destin is so popular in the first place/
